Filter Relations Based upon one Node


Right click on a desired node. You will see a drop down menu as shown in Node Filtering by Depth. To filter by incoming, outgoing, mutual,

or undirected links, click on ‘Show Selected Link.’ This window below will appear. Check the ties you would like to show and click 'Show'. The network will be filtered accordingly.



Filter Relations Based upon multiple Nodes


Select multiple desired nodes (see Node Selection and Deselection). Right click on one of the nodes, the window

below will appear. Choose to filter all links, mutual links, or undirected links, then click on ‘Show’.
